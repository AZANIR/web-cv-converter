## The Challenge

## What I Did
[Describe your approach in 3–5 bullet points.]

- Audited the existing campaign structure and identified $3k/month in wasted spend
- Rebuilt campaigns using SKAG structure with tightly themed ad groups
- Added 200+ negative keywords to eliminate irrelevant traffic
- Implemented conversion tracking properly (was broken before)
- A/B tested ad copy across 3 variants per ad group

---

## Results
[Quantified outcomes — always use numbers where possible.]

- 📈 ROAS improved from 1.2x → 3.8x within 60 days
- 💰 Reduced wasted ad spend by 37%
- 📉 CPA dropped from $42 → $18
- ✅ Client renewed for an additional 6-month contract

## What Made the Difference
[1–2 sentences on the key insight or strategy that drove results.]
