# Case Study: Manual QA for AI-Powered Image Generation SaaS (NDA)

## Overview
- **Client type:** AI SaaS platform
- **Industry:** AI / Creative Tech (image generation & editing)
- **Service provided:** QA Manual Tester — exploratory testing, AI output validation, payment QA
- **Tags:** Manual Testing, AI Testing, Prompt Validation, Jira, Functional Testing, Payment Testing, Exploratory Testing, Regression Testing, UX Testing
---

## The Challenge
A SaaS platform enabling users to generate and edit images through text prompts needed thorough manual QA across three critical areas: AI output consistency, payment gateway reliability, and cross-platform authentication. Automated testing couldn't adequately cover the subjective and variable nature of AI-generated content.

Key pain points:
- AI image outputs were inconsistent — problematic prompt inputs weren't isolated or documented
- Payment gateway had edge cases causing transaction failures across different methods
- Login and sign-up flows behaved inconsistently across browsers and devices, generating support tickets
- No structured approach to validating prompt-driven AI features at scale

---

## What I Did
- Led comprehensive manual QA covering exploratory, boundary, regression, and usability testing
- Designed and executed **2250+ image prompt test scenarios** to identify AI edge cases and validate output consistency
- Built structured **prompt validation flows** to systematically isolate and document problematic inputs
- Verified **50+ payment scenarios** across methods and edge cases — identified and resolved 10 major gateway bugs
- Developed a **cross-browser and cross-device testing matrix** for authentication flows (login, sign-up, session handling)
- Logged **220+ detailed Jira issues** with reproduction steps, severity classification, and environment specs
- Delivered structured UI/UX feedback leading to 25+ interface improvements
- Maintained full test documentation in **Jira and Google Sheets**

---

## Results
- **2250+ prompt scenarios tested**, surfacing critical AI output edge cases
- **220+ bugs logged**, improving platform stability and performance
- **10 major payment gateway bugs resolved** through boundary and simulation testing
- **30% reduction in support tickets** from improved authentication flows
- **25+ UI/UX enhancements** shipped based on usability feedback

---

## What Made the Difference
Treating AI output testing with the same rigor as traditional functional testing — structured prompt categories, documented edge cases, reproducible failure scenarios — gave the dev team actionable data instead of vague "the AI did something weird" reports. That structure turned subjective QA into traceable bugs.
